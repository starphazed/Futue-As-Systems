Glossary of Terms

Understanding key terms in the privacy and data protection field will help you navigate this complex topic. Here are some common terms:

Surveillance Capitalism: The practice of collecting data from users to predict and modify behavior, often for commercial purposes.

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): A European Union regulation aimed at protecting user privacy by restricting how businesses can collect and use personal data.

Encryption: The process of converting information into a secure format that can only be read by those with the decryption key.

Anonymization: The process of removing personal identifiers from data to prevent the identification of individuals.
